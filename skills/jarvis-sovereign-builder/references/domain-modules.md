# Domain Modules

## Autonomous digital agency and SaaS factory
Market Discovery, Acquisition, Delivery, Customer Operations and Financial Control. Require lawful sourcing, consent/opt-out, realistic acquisition cost, capacity, gross margin, churn/refund risk, cash flow, workload and evidence. Revenue targets are objectives, not guarantees.

## Trading
Separate from agency and personal finance. Require data quality, backtests, walk-forward/out-of-sample validation, paper/shadow stages, slippage/fees, risk engine, sizing/exposure limits, broker adapters, reconciliation, kill switch, tax exports, attribution and retirement rules. Progress research -> out-of-sample -> paper -> shadow -> tiny capital -> controlled scale. No guaranteed profits, martingale, hidden leverage or autonomous risk-limit changes.

## AI CFO
Bank/ledger reconciliation, AR/AP, cash flow, budgets, scenarios, profitability, anomalies, supplier optimisation, tax evidence, reports, covenant/liquidity, capital recommendations, fraud/duplicate alerts and audit evidence. Professional approval remains for lodgements, audits, regulated advice, insolvency, material transactions and director declarations.

## Health intelligence
Record organisation, medication lists, reminders, symptom journals, wearable trends, clinician questions, evidence summaries, care tasks, coordination, emergency escalation and consent-controlled family access. Never assert certain diagnosis, alter prescriptions, ignore emergencies, conceal uncertainty, reuse data without consent or replace clinicians/emergency services.

## Property buyer capture
Capture, classify, verify, deduplicate, score and structure potential buyers. Remain capture-only unless outreach is separately requested and authorized. Preserve source, consent, provenance, confidence, contact restrictions and audit trail.

## XRP/HBAR and market intelligence
Research-first/paper-first unless live authority is separately proven. Separate verified facts from scenarios and speculation; track official sources, market/on-chain evidence, regulation, catalysts, risks, assumptions and probability changes. No guaranteed price outcome.

## Bill-CFO/source intake
Prioritize source readability, OCR, provenance, deduplication, privacy, evidence registers, professional gates and zero-loss handoff across financial, tax, SMSF, legal-adviser and uploaded-file workflows.
