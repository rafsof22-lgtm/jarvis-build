1. Preserve raw archive hashes and immutable local pointers.

### P4 — Cross-module staging and E2E proof
Prove federation, privacy, security, persistence, idempotency, retries, accessibility, observability, backup/restore, disaster recovery and cost limits.

### P5 — Controlled production promotion
Require explicit provider, credential, cost and production approval, then canary, monitor and preserve rollback.

### P6 — Final reconciliation and acceptance
Every accessible requirement must be implemented and verified or explicitly waived with residual risk. Produce the release manifest and obtain owner acceptance.
